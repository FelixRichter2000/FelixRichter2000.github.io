Introduction

This site is for analysing algos on Terminal.
If you are new to this, try the folling steps to get to know this site:
  1. Click on the Leaderboard Tab and wait until it has loaded completely (this can take a while)
  2. This Leaderboard shows many algos sorted by ranking (multiple per player and even ones that are not active anymore).
  3. Now you can click on a username and you will be directed to the 'Overview Player' Tab. Wait until it has loaded. Then you can see all the matches from all the algos from the player you clicked on.
  4. Green cells represent wins, Red cells represent looses. When you click on them you will be redirected to the replay of the match.
